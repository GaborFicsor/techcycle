Product Conditions

All of our products are pre-owned, and their condition reflects their previous use. While we make every effort to accurately assess each item, it's important to understand that these devices are not brand new. However, we make sure to inspect as thoroughly as we can to meet our customers standards and provide an affordable alternative to buying brand new products.

At TechCycle, we offer products in various conditions to meet the needs of our customers. Each product is thoroughly inspected and categorized into one the following conditions:

Acceptable

These items have noticeable signs of wear and may show scratches, scuffs, or minor dents. Despite their appearance, they are fully functional and come with all essential features.

Good

Items in good condition are in decent shape with some visible wear. They may have light scratches or small dents but are fully functional and ready to use. These itmes come in their original packaging which may show signs of wear.

Excellent

These items are in exceptional condition and appear like new. They have very minimal signs of previous use and each come in it's original packaging.

If you need more specific information about any of our second-hand products, please feel free to reach out to our customer support team. We are here to assist you in making a good decision.
