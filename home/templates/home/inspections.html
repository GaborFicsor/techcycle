Product Inspections

Before offering our second-hand products for sale, we do a comprehensive testing process to ensure functionality and performance. Here are some details about the testing process

Functionality:

We ensure that all of our products's essential functions are working correctly. Every product undergoes a thorough inspection to test features such as power on/off, screen display, buttons, ports, speakers and microphones.

Performance:

We assess the overall performance of each product, icluding their speed, responsiveness and handling various tasks. We want to offer a good standard of products that our customers can trust.

Software:

We verify installed softwares on the devices to make sure there are no harmful data or software is present. We make every necessary or possible software updates to make our products as up-to date as possible. If necessary, we perform a factory reset for optimal performance.

Hardware:

We carefully inspect every hardware components, including processors, memory, storages and graphics capabilities. Where possible we test connectivity and water resistancy of small devices.

Battery:

We examine all of our product's battery health percentage and if possible and necessary, we change the battery to make our devices even more ready to go.

Appearance:

We pay attention to our products conditions and appearances as much as we can. Where necessary we replace parts that are weary and clean components that are dusty.

With all these tests we aim to provide fully functional second-hand products, ready for their next adventure.
